Outcome 1

1.1 - What is the purpose of IT in modern business

IT can be used for modern business in many ways. For example they can be used for data analysis, to track how customers are using or interacting with the business, how much money is being earned, spent etc., how the company is progressing and what changes can be made to improve it. IT can also be used to create user friendly websites and apps for the company to display what they do, and to process payments.

In fact, many companies can become completely IT based. Many financial companies are already harnessing IT. These companies are known as "Fintech’s" combining “finance” and “technology”; they harness technology to enhance or automate financial services and processes.

Risks in projects are caused mainly by human error, the slightest of mistakes in any code can cause the whole project to fail. Not saving progress also poses a problem, where valuable time and information can be lost. Security also is of importance, to ensure the project is safe.

1.7 - Legal guidelines that impact digital projects

There are regulations that must be followed when storing data, known as General data protection regulations, or GDPR. GDPR requires you to be more thoughtful about the sites and services you build, more transparent about the ways you collect and use data, more considerate of your users, and more thorough in your development and documentation processes.
